About WoodCraft Masters

25+ Years of Excellence in Carpentry & Woodwork

Our Story

Founded in 1998 by Master Carpenter Mr. Rajesh Verma, WoodCraft Masters began as a small workshop with a big dream - to create furniture that tells a story. What started as a one-man operation has now grown into a team of 15 skilled craftsmen, each carrying forward our legacy of excellence.

Over the past 25 years, we've served over 5000 satisfied customers, transforming their homes and offices with our handcrafted wooden creations. Our journey is built on three core principles: quality craftsmanship, customer satisfaction, and timeless design.
